Garden apothecary

Herbology

Plant magic, remedies, and garden wisdom.

Step into the living garden and learn the language of herbs, flowers, roots, and moonlit remedies. Save plants to your Pixie memory, collect botanical charms, and unlock recipes for teas, tinctures, spells, and altar offerings.

Safety first
Natural does not mean harmless.
Plants and extracts can interact with medicines, affect surgery risk, irritate skin, stress the liver, or be unsafe in pregnancy. This page teaches what to check before using an herb.
Pixie lane
Education, not plant identification.
Pixie Portal should not tell people to ingest wild plants. The useful product is clear wellness info: evidence notes, common forms, interactions, and who should avoid each herb.
Before using an herb or supplement

Check the plant identity, plant part, preparation, dose/duration, medication interactions, pregnancy/breastfeeding status, liver/kidney conditions, upcoming surgery, and product quality. For exposures or suspected poisoning in the U.S., call Poison Control at 1-800-222-1222.
